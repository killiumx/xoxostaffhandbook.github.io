Advanced EMS & Medical Roleplay

Comprehensive medical system with realistic procedures and consequences

🏥 Advanced Medical Roleplay
Our server features a comprehensive medical roleplay system including realistic pregnancy care, advanced illness treatment, surgical procedures, and a fully integrated prescription system.
👶
Pregnancy & Maternal Care
Comprehensive prenatal care system with realistic monitoring and consequences.
  • 3-5 pre-pregnancy checkups
  • Nutrition and exercise guidance
  • Special monitoring during pregnancy
  • Realistic consequences for improper care
🏥
Illness & Treatment System
Realistic medical conditions with visual symptoms and proper treatment requirements.
  • Visual symptoms (coughing, limping, etc.)
  • Advanced diagnostic tools
  • Interactive Medical Guidebook
  • Contagious illness spread
🔬
Advanced Surgery System
Innovative surgical procedures with hand targeting and realistic outcomes.
  • Hand targeting for props interaction
  • 5 custom surgeries (Heart, Kidney, Lung, Arm, Leg)
  • Surgical tools: Scalpel, Saw, Syringe
  • Training mode available
  • High-stakes: mistakes can be fatal
💊
Prescription System
Realistic prescription system with health insurance integration.
  • Realistic prescription pad UI
  • Doctor fills fields manually
  • Pharmacy dispensing system
  • Health insurance reduces costs
  • Correct prescriptions cure, wrong ones worsen
⚖️
Medical Facilities & Services
State-of-the-art medical facilities providing comprehensive care.
  • Pediatrics center for child care
  • Daycare system for children
  • Advanced surgical procedures
  • Interactive roleplay requirements
  • Ambulance transport with stretchers
👨‍⚖️
Court System & Child Support
Legal framework for family matters and child welfare cases.
  • Child labor and welfare cases
  • Child support enforcement
  • Neglect consequences
  • Interactive court proceedings
