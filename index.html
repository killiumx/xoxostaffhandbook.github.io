Handling Sits
Follow proper procedures when dealing with player issues.
  • Always open a sit before warning players
  • Handle all sits at a sit location which will be given
  • List every rule broken when punishing
  • Gather sufficient evidence before bans
  • Don't handle sits involving friends/clan
  • Mute toxic players, don't escalate

  • Don't make anonymous staff reports
  • Don't message players in DMs about issues
  • Outside behavior affects standing if harmful
  • Keep internal discussions private
  • Maintain professionalism everywhere
