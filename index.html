Server Ownership

Owners (3)
Hidden, ultimate authority over the entire FiveM server. Maintain complete oversight of all operations, infrastructure, and governance while remaining behind the scenes.
⚠️ Important Note
Ownership operates behind the scenes and rarely intervenes in day-to-day governance, allowing democratic processes to function independently.

Executive Leadership

Director / President
Elected
Highest elected official serving 2-week terms. Leads the city government, sets policy direction, and represents all citizens.
Term & Selection
  • 2-week term (renewable through re-election)
  • Elected by player vote

City Sectors

📋 Sector Overview
All city sectors operate under government supervision to ensure proper licensing, compliance, and service to citizens.
💼 Business Sector
Business Operations
The economic engine of the city, encompassing all commercial enterprises.
Key Points
  • All businesses require government licenses
  • Admins approve new businesses and monitor compliance
  • Includes shops, restaurants, dealerships, and services
⚙️ Industrial Sector
Industrial Operations
Manual labor and resource production jobs.
Details
  • Jobs: Mining, factories, non-whitelisted labor
  • Overseen by: Director of Industry & Resources (Appointed)
  • Open to all citizens without application
🚨 Emergency Services
Police Department
Law enforcement agency maintaining order and safety.
Leadership & Operations
  • Leadership: Chief of Police (Government Appointed)
  • Whitelisted positions requiring applications
  • Handles crime prevention, investigations, and arrests
Emergency Medical Services
Medical response team providing healthcare and emergency treatment.
Leadership & Operations
  • Leadership: Chief of EMS
  • Whitelisted medical professionals
  • Responds to injuries, accidents, and medical emergencies
Fire Department
Fire suppression and rescue operations.
Leadership & Operations
  • Leadership: Fire Chief
  • Whitelisted firefighters and rescue personnel
  • Handles fires, vehicle accidents, and rescue operations
Department of Justice (DOJ)
Legal system managing courts, prosecution, and judicial proceedings.
Leadership & Operations
  • Leadership: Attorney General / Chief Justice
  • Whitelisted judges, attorneys, and legal staff
  • Handles trials, legal cases, and constitutional matters
  • Prosecutes criminals and defends citizens' rights
🎭 Community & Civic Engagement
Community Programs
Public-facing initiatives to enhance citizen experience.
Activities
  • Public events managed by Event Staff
  • Community programs overseen by Community Coordinator
  • Citizen engagement initiatives and town halls

Citizens / Players

Employed Citizens
Players actively working in various sectors of the economy.
Employment Categories
  • Business: Shop owners, employees, managers
  • Industrial: Miners, factory workers, laborers
  • Government: Administrative staff, officials
  • Emergency Services: Police, EMS, Fire Department
Unemployed / Gang Roles
Citizens not formally employed or involved in criminal organizations.
Roleplay Options
  • Freeform roleplay: Create your own story and activities
  • Gang members: Organized crime groups with territory
  • Monitored by: EMS and Police departments
  • Can engage in legal or illegal activities (subject to RP rules)
ℹ️ For New Players
All players start as citizens and can apply for positions in any sector. Some roles require applications and interviews (whitelisted jobs), while others are open to all. Explore the city, meet people, and find your path!

EMS & Medical Roleplay System

🏥 Advanced Medical Roleplay
Our server features a comprehensive medical roleplay system including realistic pregnancy care, advanced illness treatment, surgical procedures, and a fully integrated prescription system.
Pregnancy & Maternal Care
Comprehensive prenatal care system with realistic monitoring and consequences.
Features
  • 3–5 pre-pregnancy checkups with doctor follow-ups
  • Learning sessions on nutrition, exercise (yoga), and lifestyle
  • Special guidance and monitoring during pregnancy
  • Realistic consequences: improper care can result in loss
Medical Facilities & Services
State-of-the-art medical facilities providing comprehensive care.
Available Services
  • Pediatrics center for EMS and child care
  • Daycare system for children
  • Advanced surgical procedures by doctors
  • Relatives/assistants can interact and help patients
  • Environmental injuries, accidents, infections managed realistically
Illness, Injuries & Treatment
Realistic medical conditions with visual symptoms and proper treatment requirements.
System Features
  • Visual symptoms: coughing, sneezing, limping, vomiting, etc.
  • Illnesses persist until correctly treated
  • Advanced tools: Pulse Oximeter, Thermometer, Blood Pressure Cuff, MRI
  • Interactive Medical Guidebook for diagnosis
  • Correct prescriptions cure; wrong prescriptions worsen conditions
  • Contagious illnesses spread if untreated
Roleplay & Interaction
High-stakes medical roleplay with realistic consequences and interactions.
Interaction Requirements
  • Doctors must use /me and /do commands for interactions
  • Ambulance transport with stretcher hand-off for surgeries
  • Physical therapy required for certain treatments (e.g., crutches)
  • High-stakes: mistakes can be fatal with realistic consequences
Advanced Surgery System
Innovative surgical procedures with hand targeting and realistic outcomes.
Surgery Features
  • Innovative Hand Targeting: Use hands to grab and interact with props
  • 5 Custom Surgeries: Heart, Kidney, Lung, Arm, Leg (expandable)
  • Surgical Tools: Scalpel, Saw, Syringe
  • Perform on real players (supports dead players)
  • Failing surgery kills the player - retry or leave
  • Training Mode available for practice
Prescription & Health Insurance System
Realistic prescription system with health insurance integration.
How It Works
  • Players receive medicine from illness or doctor RP
  • Prescription Pad: Realistic UI (UK, US, or Germany style)
  • Doctors fill fields manually, select medicine and dosage
  • Patients view full prescription details
  • Pharmacy: NPC or player pharmacist dispenses medicine
  • Health Insurance: Reduces or eliminates medicine costs
  • Insurance duration configurable (temporary or indefinite)
Court System & Child Support
Legal framework for family matters and child welfare cases.
Legal System
  • Courts handle child labor and welfare cases
  • Child support rules enforced for parents/guardians
  • Neglect or illegal activity results in fines/legal consequences
  • Interactive roleplay for proceedings and verdicts
